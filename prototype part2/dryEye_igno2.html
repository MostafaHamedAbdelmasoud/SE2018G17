Hair loss in women

If you suffer from eye dryness and dry mouth, the cause may be a disease called sjogren's syndrome, an immune disease in which the body's immune system attacks certain tissues in the body, including the glands that produce tears and saliva. The following question asks about the advantages of this disease that make it more likely in your case. Along with eye dryness and dry mouth, other things are common in people with schogren's syndrome

    In addition to eye dryness and dry mouth, do you suffer from one or more of the following?
  • Swelling of parotid gland - gland located in the side of the face down and in front of the ear directly.
  • Negative results for tears production tests, or saliva by your doctor, ophthalmologist or ENT. These tests include: Schirmer's test, Rose-Bengal staining test, normal saliva flow, stimulation and lip sample analysis.
  • Antibody antibody testing is positive (abnormal), especially with one antibody type, of an anti-RoA antibody.

If you are not sure that you are experiencing any of these things, you can get information from your doctor's office.
